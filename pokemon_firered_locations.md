# Pokémon FireRed: Complete Catchable Pokémon & Location Guide

This guide provides a comprehensive list of all Pokémon available to obtain and catch in **Pokémon FireRed**. It includes their standard routes, caves, special encounter mechanics, and specific post-game Sevii Islands locations. 

*Note: Pokémon exclusive to LeafGreen (such as Sandshrew, Vulpix, Bellsprout, Magmar, Pinsir, Azurill/Marill, Misdreavus, Sneasel, Remoraid, and Mantine) are omitted or noted as unavailable to catch directly in FireRed without trading.*
